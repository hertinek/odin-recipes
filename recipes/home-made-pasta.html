Home-made Pasta

Description

Home-made pasta is not so difficult.

Ingredients

  • 400 g white flour
  • 4 eggs
  • almond milk (yes)
  • salt (in the water)

Steps

  1. Make a mount with the flour on a table and create a hole in the middle
  2. Break the eggs inside this hole, beat them gently with a fork and slowly add flour from the rig of the mount
  3. Finish combining with hands, adding some almond milk until you obtain a homogeneous, elastic dough that doesn't stick
  4. Wrap in a clean cloth and let rest for half-an-hour in the fridge
  5. Take off pieces from the dough and shape it the way you like with a pasta machine
  6. When it's all done, pour the pasta in two liters of boiling water and taste from time to time until it's good, and serve
